Pumpkin Turkey Chili Recipe.

Description

Pumpkin Turkey Chili is a delightful twist on the classic chili recipe, combining the rich, earthy flavors of pumpkin with lean ground turkey. This hearty and comforting dish offers a perfect balance of sweet and savory notes, with the pumpkin adding a subtle sweetness that complements the savory spices and ground turkey. It's a great way to incorporate the goodness of pumpkin into a savory dish, making it a perfect choice for autumn or any time you want a warm, satisfying meal.

The addition of pumpkin to this chili not only enhances its flavor but also contributes a creamy texture, making it a heartwarming and comforting option for chilly evenings. With a medley of traditional chili ingredients like beans, tomatoes, and spices, this Pumpkin Turkey Chili is a wholesome and nutritious choice, offering a hearty dose of protein and fiber. Whether you're looking for a seasonal twist on a classic dish or simply seeking a nutritious, one-pot meal, Pumpkin Turkey Chili is a flavorful and nourishing option that's sure to please your taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil.
  • 1 cup chopped onion.
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper.
  • ½ cup chopped yellow bell pepper.
  • 1 clove garlic, minced.
  • 1 pound ground turkey.
  • 2 cups pumpkin puree.
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chili powder or more to taste.
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper.
  • 1 dash salt.
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese.
  • ½ cup sour cream.
    •

    Steps:

    1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, green bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, and garlic; cook and stir until tender.
    2. Stir in turkey and cook until evenly brown; drain.
    3. Mix in tomatoes and pumpkin. Season with chili powder, pepper, and salt. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 15 to 20 minutes. Serve topped with Cheddar cheese and sour cream.
